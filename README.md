# Amazon-sales-Dashboard
Designed an interactive dashboard to analyze sales, profit, and order trends across months,  categories, and regions. Performed data cleaning, transformation, and modeling using Excel functions and DAX  formulas to ensure accuracy. Created KPIs and visual reports to track performance, top products, and regional growth  insights.
This project involved analyzing a comprehensive dataset containing 5,901 sales records from Amazonâ€™s U.S. retail operations. The dataset captured key transactional details such as order information, customer demographics, shipping methods, product categories, financial metrics, and payment patterns. Below is a structured summary of the insights derived from the analysis:

1. Dataset Overview

Total Records: 5,901

Total Features: 21

Data Fields Included: Order and ship details, customer attributes, geographic information, product details, sales, profit, quantity, discount, returns, and payment mode.

2. Sales & Profit Analysis

Average Sales per Transaction: $230

Maximum Recorded Sale: $9,099

Average Profit: $28

Maximum Profit: $8,399

Insight: Sales figures indicate the presence of both regular consumer goods and high-value items, especially in categories like Furniture and Technology. Profit margins generally remain moderate with occasional high-profit outliers.

3. Shipping Mode Distribution

Predominant Mode: Standard Class

Other Modes: First Class, Second Class, Same Day

Insight: Standard Class shipping is the most utilized option, implying a customer preference for cost-effective delivery. Premium shipping modes are used for time-sensitive orders.

4. Customer Segmentation

Key Segments: Consumer, Corporate, Home Office

Insight: The Consumer segment appears to be the dominant customer base, followed by Corporate buyers.

5. Geographic Distribution

Orders span across U.S. regions: East, West, Central, South

Insight: The dataset represents a well-distributed customer base across major U.S. regions, supporting broad market coverage.

6. Product Category Insights

Primary Categories: Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology

Sub-Categories include: Chairs, Bookcases, Phones, Accessories, Binders, Storage, etc.

Insight: The product mix covers both high-value durable goods and everyday office essentials.

7. Return Rate

Average Return Rate: 4.86%

Most orders are not returned, indicating generally positive customer satisfaction or product alignment.

8. Payment Mode Distribution

Includes modes such as Cash on Delivery (COD), Online Payment, and Card.

Most Frequently Used: COD

Insight: COD is a major payment preference among customers, emphasizing convenience and trust-based purchasing.
